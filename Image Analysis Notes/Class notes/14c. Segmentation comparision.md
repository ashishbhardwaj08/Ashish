
# Most Importan

|Type|Methods|
|---|---|
|**Texture-based**|GLCM|
|**Clustering (unsupervised)**|K-Means, GMM|
|**Region-based**|Region Growing, Split & Merge|
|**Thresholding**|Histogram-based, Otsu|
|**Edge-based**|Canny|

---

# 1. GLCM (Gray Level Co-occurrence Matrix)
 **Type:** Texture-based

- Uses **spatial relationship of pixels**
- Works on **patches (windows)**

### Idea:

“How often do pixel pairs occur?”

### Good for:

- Texture segmentation (grass vs road)

### Bad for:

- Simple objects with uniform intensity

---

# 2. K-Means

👉 **Type:** Clustering (hard clustering)

- Assigns each pixel to **one cluster only**

### Idea:

Minimize distance to cluster centers

###  Good for:

- Fast segmentation
- Color/intensity grouping

### Bad for:

- Overlapping data
- Noise sensitive

---

# 3. GMM (Gaussian Mixture Model)

**Type:** Probabilistic clustering (soft clustering)

- Each pixel has **probability of belonging to clusters**

###  Idea:

Data = mixture of Gaussians

### Good for:

- Complex distributions
- Overlapping regions

### Bad for:

- Slower than K-means

---

## K-Means vs GMM (IMPORTANT)

|Feature|K-Means|GMM|
|---|---|---|
|Assignment|Hard|Soft|
|Shape|Spherical clusters|Any Gaussian shape|
|Speed|Fast|Slower|
|Accuracy|Lower|Higher|

---

#  4. Region Growing

 **Type:** Region-based

### Idea:

Start from seed → grow similar pixels

### Good for:

- Connected regions

### Bad for:

- Seed selection critical
- Noise sensitive

---

# 5. Split and Merge

 **Type:** Region-based

### Idea:
  
- Split image → small regions
- Merge similar ones

### Good for:

- Structured segmentation

### Bad for:

- Computationally heavy

---

# 6. Histogram-Based Thresholding
 **Type:** Thresholding

### Idea:

Use histogram peaks to separate objects

### Good for:

- Simple foreground/background

### Bad for:

- Poor lighting conditions

---

# 7. Otsu’s Method

👉 **Type:** Automatic thresholding

### Idea:

Chooses threshold that minimizes variance
 Formula concept:

- Minimize **intra-class variance**

### Good for:

- Automatic thresholding

### Bad for:

- Multi-object scenes

---

# 8. Watershed

 **Type:** Morphological / region-based

###  Idea:

Image = topographic surface  
→ Flood from minima

### Good for:

- Touching objects (coins, cells)

###  Bad for:

- Over-segmentation

---

#  9. Canny Edge Detection
 **Type:** Edge-based

### Steps:

1. Noise removal
2. Gradient detection
3. Non-max suppressio
4. Hysteresis thresholding

### Good for:

- Sharp edges

### Bad for:

- Not full segmentation (only edges)

---

# Final Comparison

|Method|Uses|Output|
|---|---|---|
|GLCM|Texture|Texture regions|
|K-Means|Clustering|Pixel groups|
|GMM|Probabilistic clustering|Soft segmentation|
|Region Growing|Similar pixels|Connected regions|
|Split & Merge|Recursive regions|Structured segmentation|
|Histogram|Intensity|Binary image|
|Otsu|Auto threshold|Binary image|
|Watershed|Topology|Segmented regions|
|Canny|Edges|Boundaries|
